The Prophecy Comes True

What Happens in the Book?

Percy goes back to camp where he is met with a celebration from the campfor the completion of the quest. He also receives a letter from his mom that insinuates that she used Medusa's head and sold the sculpture which gave her enough money to move and to start college. She also gives him the option of returning to go to a private school for 7th grade.

Percy makes it to the end of the summer and has to decide whether he will stay year-round or live with his mom. Grover already received his searcher's license and left weeks ago and Annabeth will stay year-round. Before he makes the decision, he goes to training area to practice but finds Luke there. Luke invites him to the woods to fight a something and Percy agrees.

Percy realizes the third part of the prophecy was about Luke. Luke has grown to hate the gods and was manipulated by Kronos.Percy learns that Luke stole the lightening Bolt and the helm. Ares found out what he did and was going to turn him in but Luke waas able to manipulate him into hiding the items until another demigod could take it to Hades. That demigod was Percy but their plan failed. He summons a scorpion to kill Percy. Luke leaves and the scorpion attacks. Percy is able to kill it but not before he is poisoned. He is able to get help and loses conciousness.

When Percy wakes up Annabeth and Chiron are there helping him recover. He tells them about what Luke told him. Chiron leaves for Olympus to tell the gods and tells Percy he must decide if he will stay at camp or go. Before he decides Percy sends Annabeth off. She decided to live with her family for the year. She promises to help look for Luke with Percy when she returns. Percy then decides to leave camp to try to live with his mom.

Main Takeaways

  • Camp Half Blood is a summer camp meant for demigods to protect them from monsters that want to kill them.

Who We Meet

  • Argus: Camp Security
  • Mr.D(Dionysius): God of Wine who's punishment is to be camp director at Camp Half-Blood
