I Settle My Tab

What Happens in the Book?

The mist changes the three delinquents into three kidnapped children, taken by Ares. They use the sob story to raise money to make it to New York. The three separate at the airport. Percy goes to the empire state building and the others go back to camp to tell Chiron what happened.

At the empire state building, the security guard gives Percy a card that gives him access to the 600th floor. At the topo he travels through Olympus to meet the gods. He explains what happened and describes the dreams he had to give clues as to who could be behind the entire issue. He gives Zeus his bolt and Zeus halts all mention of the individual behind the scheme. Percy believes that Kronos is being awakened but leaves the issue alone.

Percy is able to have a short conversation with his father where he expresses his approval. He also tells Percy that Hades returned his mother. His father tells him to return home to retrieve something and make a choice.

Percy makes it back to the apartment and is reunited with his mom. Gabe is there as welland is demanding meatloaf from Sally. He moves to hit her and Percy realizes that Gabe has abused her before. Sally takes an angry Percy to his room where he finds the package that he sent to Olympus. He realizes that this is part of the choice that he has to make. He offers to turn Gabe to stone but Sally convinces him not to, claiming that she must live her own life. Percy agrees but not before telling Sally that he will go back to camp and make his decision to come back depending on whether Gabe stays.
