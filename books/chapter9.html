What Happens in the Book?

Now that everyone knows who Percy's godly parent is, his now moved to the the Poseidon cabin. Finding out who is father is, is bittersweet because now everyone is avoiding him. When he thought that he was finally fitting in, everyones seems to be now scared to approach him.

Over a game of Pinochle, he learns that Greek gods are real and are now based in America. All of this info was hard for Percy to believe but I think he is convinced when he see Chiron get out of his wheelchair and into his real form.
