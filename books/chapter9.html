I Am Offered a Quest

What Happens in the Book?

Now that everyone knows who Percy's godly parent is, he his now moved to the the Poseidon cabin(Cabin 3). Finding out who is father is, is bittersweet because now everyone is avoiding him. When he thought that he was finally fitting in, everyones seems to be now scared to approach him. Especially since it seems that someone is trying to harm Percy and is able to gain access to the camp to do so.

After some time passes, Grover informs Percy that he is being summoned by Mr.D. They go to the main house to find Mr.D and Chiron playing pinochle with two invisible opponents. Mr.D leaves to participate in an emergency meeting with the other gods. After he leaves, Chiron discusses with Percy the turmoil with the gods at the moment. Percy mentions his dreams and how he thinks that his father and uncle are fighting over something. Chiron acknowledges this and explains to Percy that they are looking for a lightning bolt, Zeus's master bolt specifically.

Zeus believes that Percy took the bolt during the winter solstice while the gods were meeting. Percy vehemently denies taking the bolt as he has never been to Olympus. Chiron believes he knows who has the bolt and offers Percy a quest in order to retrieve it. With the deadline to get the lightning bolt being the summer solstice, Percy accepts the quest and is told to speak to the Oracle.

Percy goes upstairs and speaks to the Oracle where she orates the prophecy. Percy repeats the first two lines to Chiron and Grover. He fails to say the last two lines because he thinks the lines mean something will go wrong on the quest. Chiron is sceptical that Percy is not saying everything but does not press him for more. Chiron proceeds to explain to Percy that he believes that Hades is the one behind the entire schemes since he is the one with most obvious gain if his brothers fight. This means that Percy has to to the Underworld. Percy now has to find the entrance to the Underworld in Los Angeles with Grover and Annabeth, who volunteered to go on the trip.

Dream Alert

Percy dreams about two men fighting on the beach which symbolize Zeus and Poseidon. Percy tries to stop them but is unable to do so He also dreams about a voice beckoning him to go underground. In the beginning, I thought he was dreaming about Hades but in actuallity, we will learn that he is dreaming about someone else. In the end, Percy falls into a crack in the earth and wakes up.

Main Takeaways

  • Percy learns about the quest that he has to take
  • Percy is conflicted about his father claiming him because it seems that the reason that he did so, was for Percy to help him make up with His father.
