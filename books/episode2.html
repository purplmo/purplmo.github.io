I Become Supreme Lord of the Bathroom

What Happens in the Book?

Percy is given a tour of the camp, first by Chiron and then by Annabeth. He is taken to Cabin 11 which represents Hermes, the god of travelers. At this point no one knows who his godly parent is, so at this time he will stay in Cabin 11 with the "undetermineds". Sidebar - I am really glad that Percy did something about this later on. Knowing that you are a demigod but not knowing who your godly parent is must have caused a lot of resentment in campers. He is introduced to Luke who is one of the older campers before they leave to tour the rest of the camp. Annabeth explains tells him the one thing they all share in common. The campers are demigods who has made it safely to the camp. He also learns why he was diagnosed with dyslexia and ADHD.

  • Dyslexia: His eyes are meant to read Ancient Greek.
  • ADHD: Fighter's instinct causes him to see more than the average person.

A girl named Clarisse, approaches Annabeth and Percy, and immediately start harassing Percy claiming it as an iniation for newcommers. Clarisse and two girls from the Ares cabin drag Percy to the bathroom and try to push him into the toilet bowl. However, the plumbing has other ideas and hoses the girls down and pushes them out of the bathroom and Annabeth drenched in water. This causes the Ares girls to leave in a rage and Annabeth to include Percy in on her plans for Capture the Flag.
