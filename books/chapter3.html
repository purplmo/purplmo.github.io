Grover Unexpectedly Loses His Pants

What Happens in the Book?

Now that I am rereading the book, I cannot believe how easily I accepted some of the names, Ugliano, Bobofit. I would not be surprised if Percy came up with those names himself just to be funny.

One thing that I always loved to see was Percy's relationship with his mother. It is obvious that they have a special bond.

Main Takeaways

  • Grover is a Keeper at Camp Half Blood.
  • The Fates meet Percy on the side of the road.
  • Mr.Brunner and Grover are trying to protect Percy from some kind of danger.
  • Grover continuously mentions not wanting to mess up again.
