My Mother Teaches Me Bullfighting

What Happens in the Book?

Still reeling from the fact that his best friend is a Saytr sent to help him, Percy, Sally, and Grover travel to Camp Half Blood in the storm. While traveling there a monstor known as the Minotaur is chasing them. The car that they are traveling in eventually becomes totaled, leaving Grover unconcious. They have to now travel on foot the rest of the way. While trying to protect Percy and Grover, Sally is turned to dust by the Minotaur. Devastated, Percy fights against the monster and defeats it. He is able to get Grover to the safety of camp where he collapses in front of Mr.Brunner and a new face, Annabeth.

Main Takeaways

  • Percy makes it to Camp Half Blood.
  • Percy thinks his mother is dead but she was actually kidnapped.

Who We Meet

  • Annabeth
  • Chiron(Mr.Brunner)
