I Plunge to My Death

What Happens in the Book?

They travel for two days on the train to Denver. They learn that Percy is wanted for blowing up the bus in New York. His step father, has posted a monetary reward for any info on him. Percy tells Annabeth about his dream about the man in the pit. From the description Annabeth does not believe that Hades is the man in his dreams and contemplates about who it could be.

Annabeth opens up about her family and feeling unwanted. She believes that her father never wanted her and how it got worse when he got married and had more children. Annabeth could not take it anymore and decides to run away at seven.

They stop at St. Louis where Annabeth sees the Gateway Arch and gets excited. She decided to take them on a field trip since they have a three hour wait. While at the top of the Arch they Grover and Annabeth get separated from Percy. This is where Percy encounters Echidna and Chimera.
