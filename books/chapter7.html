My Dinner Goes Up in Smoke

What Happens in the Book?

Annabeth show Percy around the camp some more. They Eventually start to talk about Olympus and contemplate why the gods are fighting. Annabeth thought Percy might know more but only knows that there is a Summer Solistice deadline that should be kept. Annabeth was able to gather that someone stole something and must bring it back to the gods before the Summer Solistice. Annabeth claims that she will ask Chiron to take Percy to the Oracle to gain more info and sends him on his way.

Percy goes back to the Hermes cabin before dinner and they have a brief conversation. Percy is able to gather that Annabeth is looking for a "special" camper that will enable her to go on a quest. With that knowledge Percy heads to eat dinner with the rest of the campers. Everyone gets their food and gives piece to the fire to offer to the gods. Percy is introduced to the entirety of the camp and begins to settle in.

Main Takeaways

  • Annabeth is desperate for a mission and thinks that Percy might be the key to her getting one.
  • The gods are upset about a missing object(lightning bolt)
  • Percy picks up on some negative emotions when speaking to Luke about the gods. This will be important later on.
