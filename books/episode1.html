I Accidentally Vaporize My Pre-algebra Teacher

What Happens in the Book?

The first few lines lays the ground work for the rest of the book if not the rest of the series. I do not know if you guys imagined who your Greek god parent would be but I gave it some thought many times. I think part of the reason is how the book starts. Percy speaking directly to the reader gaves the air that demigods lived inside our world and that we could possibly be one.

We gain some insightful background of Percy in this chapter. We learn of several characteristics of Percy that we will later on know demigods share. He is deemed as a troubled kid since he goes to a school that specializes in troubled kids, Yancy Academy. Due to his fighter gene he tends to cause accidents on field trips. He also has dyslexia and ADHD

The sixth graders are on a field trip to the Metropolitan Museum of Art led by Mr. Brunner, and Mrs.Dodds. At the museum Mr. Brunner relays a story that we would later on find important. This is the story of Kronos being defeated by the gods and his remains being scattered about. This is important because Percy will later on have to help in the prevention of Kronos' resurection.

The first time we see Percy's power is during his interaction with Nancy Bobofit while she is bullying Grover. Percy get angry and unknowingly shoots a stream of water at Nancy causing Percy to get in trouble with Mrs.Dodds.

Everything hits the fan when Mrs.Dodds asks to see Percy privately. Mrs.Dodds turns into one of the Furies and attacks Percy and tells him to confess. We later find out she is referring to the Lightning bolt. At this point Percy has no idea what she is talking about. However with the help of Mr. Brunner and his collapsable pen, Percy makes it out unscathed. The catch, no one remembers who Mrs. Dodds is or they are pretending to(**cough cough** Grover and Mr.Brunner).

Main Takeaways

  • We meet two major characters, Chiron and Grover.
  • Foreshadowing of the resurection of Kronos.
  • Percy is diagnosed with ADHD and dyslexia which is common with demigods.
  • Monsters are finding Percy and he has no idea why.
  • Mr.Brunner showed his students Roman garb. Was that a Foreshadowing for the next series?
