We Find Out The Truth Sort Of

What Happens in the Book?

The Coast Guard get them out of the water where they meet Ares who explains that he is the one that caused the lightning bolt to magically appear in the backpack when Percy made it to the Underworld.

When asking Ares questions, it becomes apparent that he was not the one that came up with this scheme. However, Ares denies not being the orchestrator of the whole scheme. Ares wanted Hades to kill Percy in the Underworld in order to cause a three way war. Now in order to stop Percy from delivering the lightning bolt, Ares plans to kill Percy. Percy makes a deal with Ares. If Percy beats Ares in battle he gets the helm and the lightning bolt. Ares agrees to the terms.

Percy is able to wound Ares which causes Ares to get angry. He goes in for the kill, but some force stops him from doing so. Ares leaves without the helm and the bolt. The Furies, who saw the entire battle agrees to take the helm back to Hades and explain that Percy was speaking the truth the entire time.

As they only have until the night to make it across the country, the trio are only able to travel by plane which might pose as a bad idea for Percy.

Main Takeaways

  • Camp Half Blood is a summer camp meant for demigods to protect them from monsters that want to kill them.
