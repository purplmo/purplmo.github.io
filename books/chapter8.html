We Capture a Flag

What Happens in the Book?

Percy is slowly fitting into the camp with the other campers. He still has no idea who his godly parent could be but throughout the chapter there are hints but they go unnotticed. He is good at canoeing and anytime he touches water he gets a rush of adrenaline. It is not until the end of the chapter when capture the flag is over that Annabeth picks up on it.

During capture the flag, Percy gets chosen to be on border patrol. He is on the same team as the Athena Cabin(Team Blue) opposing the Ares Cabin (Team Red). While on patrol Clarisse and her siblings try to take advantage of the fact that Percy is alone to get payback for the incident at the bathrooms. Percy is able to get the upperhand however, when he falls into the river and heals. Before he can continue his counterattack the Ares siblings are distracted by the fact that Luke has their flag, but they are too late and lose.

Before Team Blue could celebrate their win however, a hound attacks Percy. Luckily, he is saved by the other campers, but he is severely wounded. Annabeth, following a hunch, tells Percy to go into the water. Miraculously, his wounds start to heal and over his head a sign that he is the son of Poseidon appears.

Main Takeaways

  • Percy and the whole camp knows that he is the son of Poseidon.
