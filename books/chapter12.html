We Get Advice from a Poodle

What Happens in the Book?

After the debacle, the trio decide to camp near the disaster. While sleeping in shifts, Percy and Grover have a convo. At this point Grover tells Percy about the god Pan, the reason why Grover wants a searcher's license. This a dangerous journey that no searcher has returned alive from in thousands of years. During the conversation, Percy realizes that Grover's first mission was to escort Annabeth, Thalia, and Luke to camp. But overcame with guilt, he is unable to go into detail.

He does share his worries about the Kindly Ones. He believes that there is more to the mission that they did not know. The Kindly Ones seemed to be looking for an object rather than Percy. Grover eventually convinces Percy to go to sleep and Percy has a dream.
