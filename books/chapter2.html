The Old Ladies Knit the Socks of Death

What Happens in the Book?

Percy knows something is not right and he has even more questions after overhearing a conversation between Grover and Mr. Brunner. Why is he in danger? What are kindly ones? What deadline were they referring to. He presses Grover on the subject when they are taking the bus to Manhattan. However, Grover does not provide any answers. Only with the first mention of Camp Half Blood and Grover's role as a Keeper in form of a business card.

The bus breaks down and three old ladies at a fruit stand are knitting. Percy is confused as to why Grover seems to be scared of the ladies. We know however that the old ladies are the Fates and them cutting the yarn was a bad omen.

Main Takeaways

  • Grover is a Keeper at Camp Half Blood.
  • The Fates meet Percy on the side of the road.
  • Mr.Brunner and Grover are trying to protect Percy from some kind of danger.
  • Grover continuously mentions not wanting to mess up again.

Who We Meet
